# Example: Contested claim (calibration)

## Competing Interpretations

### Case FOR (steelmanned)
Proponents argue mechanism X drives outcome Y via pathway Z (**Probable** in animal models; **Speculative** in humans). Cite: Smith et al., 2022 (RCT, n=240, industry-funded).

### Case AGAINST (steelmanned)
Skeptics note null Phase III trial (Jones et al., 2024), surrogate endpoint without validation, and that observational association disappears after socioeconomic adjustment (**Probable** confounding). Independent replication absent.

## Bottom Line

**Unknown** for causal claim in general human population. **Speculative** for clinical utility until pre-registered primary endpoint met in adequately powered independent trial.
