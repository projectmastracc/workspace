# Example: Guidelines vs Literature (calibration)

## Guidelines vs Literature

| Guideline | Recommends | Literature | Alignment | Severity |
|-----------|------------|------------|-----------|----------|
| Hypothetical APA Practice Guideline (2019) | SSRI class as first-line for moderate MDD | Meta-analyses show small effect vs placebo (Hedges g ≈ 0.3); high placebo response; industry trial predominance | **Partial** | **Material** |

**Verdict:** Guideline direction is **Probable** for some benefit in moderate MDD, but "first-line" framing **materially exceeds** certainty when effect sizes are small and publication bias is likely. Guideline panel predates several null replications (**Speculative** whether ranking vs psychotherapy is evidence-based).

**DNR Notice:** Population-level synthesis only — not treatment advice for individuals.
