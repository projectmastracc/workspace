## Source Integrity

## Methods & Evidence Quality

- Design: Phase II RCT, double-blind — **Moderate** quality
- Primary endpoint changed post-hoc (protocol deviation) — downgrade certainty
- n=86 — underpowered for stated secondary outcomes
- Effect size: d=0.45 (95% CI 0.12–0.78) — statistically significant, modest clinical magnitude

## Inference Limits

Authors claim "robust efficacy"; data support **Probable** modest effect on primary scale in this sample only. Generalization to broader population **Speculative**.
